About

My life didn’t begin the way it looks now.

I was raised in the NYC housing projects by a single mother. By 30, I was a self-made millionaire.

This is where I unpack how that happened: with grit, urgency, and a stubborn unwillingness to accept the short end of the stick.

Along the way, I’ll share practical guides on lifestyle, ambition, and navigating rooms you weren’t born into, but still belong in.
